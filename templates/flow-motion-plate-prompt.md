# Template — prompt de MOTION PLATE (motion sem pessoa, para compor)

Usa para o take "herói" e para qualquer take com texto grande, logos, ou elementos perto
do rosto/mãos. Geras a cena **sem o creator** e compões por cima no CapCut (creator num
recorte, ou o plate em fundo/transições). Dá o look rico de keynote sem arriscar a cara
do creator. Lê primeiro `references/prompt-craft.md`.

## Estrutura a preencher (inglês, 1 por take)

```
[MOTION PLATE · ~<DUR>s · <preset>] — no person, full generated scene for compositing.
SHOT: <lente + câmara, ex.: 85mm macro, slow dolly-in, subtle orbit, rack focus>.
LIGHT: <volumetric/caustics/rim; é o que dá o "premium">.
SCENE / MOTION CONCEPT (tied to "<frase do take>"): <UMA metáfora visual concreta, cheia,
com verbo de movimento — forms / assembles / shatters / dissolves into particles /
unfolds / sweeps. Descreve o ambiente, os objetos e como transformam>.
MATERIALS/RENDER: <frosted glass, brushed metal, volumetric particles, liquid, smoke,
deep depth of field, subsurface glow, reflections, 4k hyperreal>.
COMPOSITION: leave the center/lower third <clear|usable> for the creator or final text to
be composited on top. Strong negative space where noted.
TIMING BEATS: 0-<t1>s <entra> · <t1>-<t2>s <transforma> · <t2>-<DUR>s <resolve>.
PALETTE: <hex do preset/design.md, 1 acento forte>.
CAMERA MOVE: <contínuo, lento e preciso — sem cortes bruscos>.
SFX: <opcional>.
AUDIO: subtle design sfx only, no speech, no music unless asked (voice/music added in post).
NEGATIVE: no people, no faces, no hands, no readable paragraph text, no emoji, no clipart,
no app/tool watermark, no mirrored/gibberish text, no extra colors beyond the palette,
no busy clutter.
```

## Exemplo bom (motion plate, dark-tech, take diz "código automático, joga pra sempre")

```
[MOTION PLATE · ~7s · bold-tech] — no person, full generated scene for compositing.
SHOT: 85mm macro, slow dolly-in along a dark reflective surface, shallow depth of field.
LIGHT: low-key graphite environment with electric-cyan rim light and soft caustics.
SCENE / MOTION CONCEPT (tied to "código automático, joga pra sempre"): on a black glass
desk, streams of glowing monospace code flow in from depth and condense into a single
clean key/unlock glyph that pulses once; behind it, faint perspective grid lines extend to
infinity suggesting "forever".
MATERIALS/RENDER: black glass, volumetric particles, neon glow, fine grain, reflections,
4k hyperreal, cinematic.
COMPOSITION: keep the lower third clearer for a composited caption; main action upper-mid.
TIMING BEATS: 0-2s code streams flow in · 2-5s streams condense into the unlock glyph ·
5-7s glyph pulses, grid extends, slow settle.
PALETTE: graphite #111118 background, electric cyan #22D3EE accent only.
CAMERA MOVE: continuous slow dolly-in, no cuts, precise.
SFX: low data whoosh on condense, soft sub on the pulse.
NEGATIVE: no people, no faces, no hands, no readable paragraph text, no emoji, no clipart,
no watermark, no mirrored/gibberish text, no extra colors, no clutter.
```

Depois, no CapCut: põe o creator (recortado) ou o take original por cima/ao lado, adiciona
o texto exato e o áudio original.
