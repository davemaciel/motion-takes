# Template — prompt de OVERLAY (motion sobre o creator)

Usa quando o creator continua no frame e o motion entra à volta. Risco médio: o Veo pode
espelhar/deformar. Reserva para takes simples; para texto/objetos perto do rosto usa o
`flow-motion-plate-prompt.md`. Lê primeiro `references/prompt-craft.md`.

## Estrutura a preencher (inglês, 1 por take)

```
[OVERLAY · ~<DUR>s · <preset>]
SOURCE: use the uploaded clip as the source plate. Preserve the creator exactly — face,
eyes, mouth, hands, body silhouette, cap and shirt, lighting and ORIGINAL CAMERA
ORIENTATION (never mirror or flip). Add motion ONLY in the left/right thirds and in the
background depth behind the shoulders. Never cover the face or hands.

SHOT: <lente + movimento de câmara subtil, ex.: subtle 35mm parallax, slow depth push>.
LIGHT: <ex.: soft volumetric side light, brand-color rim glow on the new elements>.
MOTION CONCEPT (tied to what is said — "<frase do take>"): <UMA metáfora visual concreta
com verbo de movimento; descreve como nasce, move e sai. Ex.: thin light-lines draw in
from the right and assemble into a minimal <ícone> that breathes, then dims out>.
MATERIALS/RENDER: <frosted glass / volumetric particles / depth of field / 4k hyperreal>.
TIMING BEATS: 0-1.5s <entra> · 1.5-4s <destaca/holds> · 4-<DUR>s <sai suave>.
PALETTE: <hex do preset/design.md, 1 acento forte>.
TYPOGRAPHY: <só se decorativo; senão "no on-screen text — text added in post">.
SFX: <opcional>.
NEGATIVE: no flat caption-only design, no single big word over the person, no emoji, no
clipart sparkles, no app/tool watermark, no mirrored or flipped text, no backwards or
gibberish letters, no warped face, no deformed hands, no extra fingers, no changing the
creator, no extra colors, no clutter.
```

## Exemplo bom (overlay, Apple-style, take diz "passei 4 dias criando uma skill")

```
[OVERLAY · ~6s · apple-clean]
SOURCE: use the uploaded clip as the source plate. Preserve the creator exactly — face,
eyes, mouth, hands, cap and shirt, lighting and original camera orientation (never mirror
or flip). Add motion only in the right third and background depth behind the shoulder.
SHOT: subtle slow depth push, shallow depth of field separating creator from background.
LIGHT: soft volumetric light sweep entering from upper right, gentle cool key.
MOTION CONCEPT (tied to "quatro dias desenvolvendo uma skill"): four thin frosted-glass
panels assemble one-by-one in the right third like a calendar building itself, each panel
catching a soft light sweep as it locks into place, then settling with a quiet glow.
MATERIALS/RENDER: translucent frosted glass, soft reflections, fine depth of field,
4k hyperreal, premium.
TIMING BEATS: 0-1.5s first panel slides in · 1.5-4s panels 2-4 stack with light sweeps ·
4-6s assembled stack holds and softly dims.
PALETTE: near-black #0D0D0D depth, off-white glass, one desaturated blue accent #0071E3.
TYPOGRAPHY: no on-screen text — the "4 DIAS" caption is added in post for legibility.
SFX: soft glassy clicks as each panel locks, low sub on the final settle.
NEGATIVE: no flat caption-only design, no single big word over the person, no emoji, no
clipart sparkles, no watermark, no mirrored or flipped text, no gibberish letters, no
warped face, no deformed hands, no extra colors, no clutter.
```

Repara: zero texto chapado, uma metáfora (calendário de vidro a montar-se), câmara, luz,
material e beats. O número "4 DIAS" vai no editor.
